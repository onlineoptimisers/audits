Services Provided by Agency

Agency will provide the following at no upfront cost to Client:

  • Custom 18-page website built on modern infrastructure (Astro/Cloudflare Pages)
  • Search engine optimization foundation (technical SEO, schema markup, directory submissions)
  • AI visibility optimization (structured data, entity building, content optimization)
  • Lead tracking system (forms, call tracking, attribution reporting)
  • Google Business Profile optimization
  • Monthly performance reporting

Agency will also set up and manage paid advertising campaigns (Google Ads, Facebook/Instagram) if Client elects to fund ad spend. Ad spend is paid directly to platforms by Client.

2
Commission Structure
Agency earns 10% of first-year contract value on every deal attributable to Agency's system.
How Commission is Calculated
Contract Type Commission Basis Example
One-time project
event security, risk assessment		 10% of total contract value $10,000 contract = $1,000 commission
Recurring service
monitoring, guard service		 10% of first 12 months of payments $2,000/month contract = $2,400 commission
Multi-year contract
long-term agreements		 10% of first-year value only $100,000/year for 3 years = $10,000 commission
When Commission is Due

Commission is payable within 30 days of Client receiving payment from the end customer. If the end customer pays in installments, commission is due on each installment as received.

3
Lead Attribution

A lead is attributable to Agency if it arrives through any of these channels:

Website Form Submission

Any form on the Agency-built website

Tracking Phone Number

Calls to the dedicated tracking number displayed on the website

Paid Advertising

Any lead generated through Agency-managed ad campaigns

Organic / AI Search

Leads who found Client through Google, ChatGPT, Perplexity, or similar platforms

Attribution Rules
Before this engagement, Client's website generates zero digital leads (site is noindexed). Any digital lead after launch is incremental.
Both parties have access to the same lead tracking dashboard.
Client must update lead status (contacted, quoted, won, lost) and deal value within 7 days of status change.
Agency provides monthly attribution report by the 5th of each month.
Client has 30 days from report date to dispute any attribution.
Call recordings are available for review in case of disputes.
What is NOT Attributable
  • ✕ Leads from Client's existing relationships or referrals predating this agreement
  • ✕ Leads from Client's own direct outreach (cold calls, in-person networking)
  • ✕ Leads from channels not built or managed by Agency
  • ✕ Walk-in business not driven by digital channels

6
Term and Termination
  • Initial Term
    12 months from effective date
  • Early Exit
    Either party may terminate with 30 days written notice after the first 90 days
  • Commission Tail
    Agency earns commission on any lead attributed during the agreement term that closes within 60 days after termination
  • Website Transfer
    Upon completion of 12-month term or mutual agreement, full website IP and hosting transfers to Client at no additional cost
  • Early Exit Site
    If Client terminates before 12 months, website remains Agency property. Client may purchase the website at fair market value.
7
Audit Rights

Agency reserves the right to request documentation (invoices, contracts, payment receipts) for any deal where commission is owed, up to once per quarter. Client will provide requested documentation within 14 days.

If audit reveals underreported revenue exceeding 10% of actual revenue, Client owes the difference plus a 15% late fee.

8
Confidentiality

Both parties agree to keep the terms of this agreement confidential. Neither party will disclose commission rates, revenue figures, or lead data to third parties without written consent.

9
Limitation of Liability

Agency does not guarantee any specific number of leads, deals, or revenue. Agency's services increase the likelihood of digital lead generation but cannot guarantee outcomes. Agency's total liability under this agreement is limited to the total commission earned in the preceding 3 months.
