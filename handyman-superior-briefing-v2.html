Unusual Discovery

The localStorage reviews are genuinely unusual. His website LOOKS like it has customer reviews - they show up when you visit. But they are stored in your browser only. A different visitor sees a blank review section. This means he cares about social proof enough to build a review system, but the implementation means zero people can actually see the reviews. Strong conversation piece.

Objection Handling

  • "How much does this cost?" - Your only cost is $500/month for the ad budget. That goes straight to Facebook. Everything else - the website, Google Maps, directories, CRM - we handle at no charge.
  • "That sounds too good to be true" - We are building our portfolio of local service businesses. Your business has strong experience and good services. We want to prove what we can do, and your success becomes our case study. (Do NOT say "test")
  • "How fast will I see results?" - Facebook ads start generating calls within 1-2 weeks. Google Maps takes about 4-6 weeks to really kick in. The ads bridge that gap so you are getting leads from day one.
  • "I'm already getting underbid by everyone" - That is exactly why online leads matter. When someone finds you through Google or Facebook, they are coming to you. You are not competing on price against 5 other bids. These are warm leads who already want a handyman.
  • "I don't have many photos" - Even phone photos of job sites work great. Before/after shots are gold. We can work with whatever you have.
