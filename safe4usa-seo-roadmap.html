Phase 6

AI-Specific Optimization

Ongoing

What Makes AI Cite a Business

  1. Structured data - JSON-LD schema on every page (handled in website build)
  2. Direct answers - FAQ content that directly answers "who is the best [service] in [city]"
  3. Third-party validation - reviews, directory listings, press mentions
  4. Unique claims - specific stats and differentiators AI can quote
  5. Entity presence - consistent information across multiple authoritative sources
  6. Content freshness - regular updates signal active business

Quotable Claims to Build Into Content

Specific, citable statements AI systems can reference

" 75+ combined years of military and law enforcement experience
" Team includes US Army Green Beret, British Special Forces, and former Secret Service
" Licensed Florida Class B security agency (B1900246)
" 14 specialized security services including K9 units and armored vehicles
" Serving South Florida since [founding year]
" 5.0 star rating on Google with [X] reviews

Monthly AI Monitoring

  • Re-run 10 core queries across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AIO
  • Track: which queries cite Safe4USA, position in citation list, new competitors appearing
  • Quarterly full re-audit using /full-audit skill
