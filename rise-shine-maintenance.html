Website & Technical Gaps

Why AI Can't Find You

Critical Website Issues

  • GoDaddy Website Builder - extremely limited for SEO. No custom schema, no blog, no URL control. AI systems struggle to crawl and index GoDaddy sites.
  • No address or phone number on your website. AI uses NAP (Name, Address, Phone) to verify and recommend local businesses.
  • Single-page site with zero real content. No service descriptions, no project case studies, no FAQ, no blog posts. AI needs text to cite.
  • Stock photos only. No real project images. Homeowners and AI both value proof of real work.
  • Typo: "Panting services" instead of "Painting services" on your booking page.
